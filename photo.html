Overview

Photography is the art of capturing moments and telling stories through images.

Benefits

Mental Health Benefits

Encourages mindfulness and improves observation skills.

Skill Development

Develops technical skills like composition and lighting.

Materials and Requirements

To get started with photography, you'll need:

  • Camera (smartphone or DSLR)
  • Editing software

Getting Started

Experiment with smartphone photography, focusing on lighting and angles.

Levels of Engagement

Move from basic snapshots to professional genres like wildlife or portrait photography.

Social and Community Aspects

Share your work on platforms like Instagram or participate in photo contests.

Fun Facts and Trivia

The first color photograph was taken in 1861 by James Clerk Maxwell!
